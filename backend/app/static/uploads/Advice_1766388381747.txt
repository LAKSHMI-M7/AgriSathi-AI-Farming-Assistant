1️⃣ Short Answer: Use fungicides and improve cultural practices to control leaf spot in bananas.

2️⃣ Detailed Guidance:
- Apply fungicides like Copper Oxychloride or Mancozeb at the first sign of leaf spot.
- Ensure proper spacing between plants for good air circulation.
- Remove and destroy infected leaves to reduce disease spread.
- Maintain proper irrigation practices; avoid overhead watering.
- Fertilize adequately to promote healthy, resilient plants.

3️⃣ Farmer Tip: Regularly monitor your banana plants for early signs of disease to take timely action.
